All about me...

Being born and raised in Gods Own Country called Kerala, I grew up as a very outgoing and active child. I immigrated to the United States with my immediate family in the hopes of accomplishing the American dream. Always staying proactive, I adventured into a lot of different activities to show my talent. Later, I realized that I am very much fond of numbers and that compelled me to take accounting classes from College of DuPage. For me, this college was a real eyeopener as I was seeking quality education without burning a hole in my pocket. Learning Commons has really helped me to conquer my math classes.
